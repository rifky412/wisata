Tiga Warna Beach

Tour Highlights

Tiga Warna Beach is one of the beaches in the CMC (Clungup Mangrove Conservation) area.
And it is another beach tourism choice when in Malang. This beach has its own uniqueness, this can be seen from its name.
Where this beach has different color gradations, and looks very beautiful from a height. Because it is in a conservation area, its cleanliness and beauty are very well maintained, just like Clungup Beach.
